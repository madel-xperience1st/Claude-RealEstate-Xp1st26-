# Demo Guide

## How to Run a PropHub Demo

### Before the Demo

1. **Verify Salesforce Data**: Ensure the target demo project has units, installments, and assets
2. **Check MuleSoft**: Confirm the Experience API is running on CloudHub
3. **Test Login**: Sign in with your presales Google account
4. **Pre-select Demo**: Navigate to the demo project to verify theming works

### Running the Demo

#### Step 1: Sign In
- Open PropHub on iPad or iPhone
- Tap "Sign in with Google"
- Select your presales Google account

#### Step 2: Select Demo Project
- You'll see a grid of available demo projects
- Each card shows the developer logo, name, and unit count
- Tap the client's project (e.g., "Emaar Beachfront")
- The app immediately re-themes with the client's colors and logo

#### Step 3: Dashboard
- Show the overview: total units, open requests, payment status
- Highlight the quick-stat cards
- Scroll to show unit summary and recent activity

#### Step 4: My Units
- Navigate to the Units tab
- Show the unit list with status badges
- Tap a unit to show floor plan and payment progress
- Demonstrate the quick-action buttons

#### Step 5: Finance
- From a unit detail, tap "View Payments"
- Show the payment summary card (total, paid, remaining)
- Walk through the installment timeline
- Switch to Invoices tab, show PDF download
- Switch to Overdue tab (if applicable)

#### Step 6: Service Requests
- Navigate to Services tab
- Show existing requests with status tracking
- Create a new request: select category, add description, attach photo
- Submit and show the new case number

#### Step 7: Assets & Warranty
- From a unit detail, tap "View Assets"
- Show the asset inventory with warranty status colors
- Tap an asset to show warranty timeline
- Switch to Maintenance tab for work order schedule

#### Step 8: Chat with Agentforce
- Navigate to Chat tab
- Demonstrate natural language queries:
  - "What's the status of my unit?"
  - "Do I have any overdue payments?"
  - "I need to fix my AC"
- Show quick-reply buttons
- Demonstrate escalation to live agent

### Switching Between Demos

- Tap the switch icon in the navigation bar from any screen
- Select a different demo project
- The entire app re-themes instantly

### Switching Salesforce Orgs

- From the Demo Switcher, tap the menu (three dots)
- Select "Org Settings"
- Add or switch to a different Salesforce org
- The app clears cached data and re-authenticates

### Tips

- Use an iPad for the best demo experience (larger screen for floor plans)
- Pre-load units and data before the demo to avoid loading delays
- Have at least one overdue installment to showcase the Overdue view
- Have at least one delivered unit with assets to showcase warranty features
- Close and reopen the app to demonstrate push notification deep linking
